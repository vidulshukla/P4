About Us
MediCare is open to all patients every day and provides fundamental medical care and cutting-edge 	
medicine in a central location in Mumbai. We use our superior academic knowledge to treat a wide 	
range of health issues, taking a personal touch and utilizing highly specialized and up-to-date 
research.

Team diversity

More than 850 employees work at MediCare. They include 150 doctors, 200 highly trained nursing 
professionals and 100 medico-therapeutic and medico-technical employees. Approximately 2,00 
employees conduct research and another 2,00 individuals provide non-medical support in areas 
such as finance, IT, facility management and catering.
